Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plank-Pro ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information. We believe in privacy by design.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The application functions completely offline. We do not transmit, store, or share any of your personal information, location data, or device identifiers with any external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data generated by your use of the app is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Timer preferences and settings
  • Workout history logs
  • Personal statistics

Because this data is stored only on your device, we have no access to it. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel) and we do not display any advertisements.
